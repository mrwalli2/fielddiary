Month-End Checklist —

The workflow

1
Log same day. Tap "Log Today", tap "Use My Location" to auto-detect the nearest facility, confirm, add notes if useful. Takes about 10 seconds.
2
Forgot a day? Use the date picker under Quick Log to backfill — don't let more than a few days pile up.
3
Snap receipts immediately. Log them in the Receipts tab the same day you get them — tap the photo field to snap it with your camera, description + amount is enough, mark "Ready" once it's actually ready to upload.
4
AT&T bill. Log it on the 1st of each month (cap: $100).
5
Month-end: Run the checklist, then hit "Copy Summary" and paste it into Concur or your notes while you enter everything.

Away-affiliate days

Your six regular locations (SRQ, NOR, CHE, FRE, BAL, DR) are always available as tap chips. When you're on the road somewhere else, tap "+ Other", type the city/opponent, and it's saved so it shows up as a quick chip next time you're there.

Keeping data in sync

Tap "Connect Google Drive" on any device — phone or desktop — and sign in once. Every entry then saves straight to a fielddiary-data.json file in your Drive, and pulls in whatever the other device has saved too (newer entries win automatically if a day exists in both). Your phone's sign-in only lasts about an hour at a time, so if the status pill says "Reconnect needed," just tap the button again — it's a one-tap re-auth, not a full reset. Receipt photos stay local to whichever device took them and aren't synced through Drive, so snap and upload to Concur from the same device.
